  • About Us

    Who we are :

    Wild Circus is a French group established in Europe and North America.The company was created by two brothers John and Kev Wild, working as an artist in a world-renowned group. It is in 2009 that desires to embark on the adventure.Their objectives: innovation, the quality and diversity of shows, customer satisfaction and the competitiveness of the offer. Wild Circuc offers a variety of shows: performers, magic, ventriloquists, fire-eaters, glown, animals, etc.

    A little story

    Date key:

    * May 2010: creation of the company in Paris. Wild Circuc was born!

    * Decmebre 2010: first show.

    * 2015, establishment in Europe: United Kingdom, Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands, Belgium, Italy, Spain, Portugal....

    * 2018, establishment in North America: Canada, United States, Mexico, Cuba.

    Today the group aims to be present everywhere in the world. Coming soon, our circuses will open in Asia then Africa.
